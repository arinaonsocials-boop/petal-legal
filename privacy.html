Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 27, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Petal ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use the Petal mobile application (the "App"). By creating an account or using the App, you consent to the practices described in this policy. We take your privacy seriously. Petal is built around the principle that your health data belongs to you, and we are committed to being transparent about how that data is handled.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. This information is used to authenticate your identity and personalize your experience within the App.

1.2 Health and Wellness Data

To provide personalized cycle insights, we collect information that you voluntarily provide, including: your menstrual cycle length, period length, and the approximate date of your last period; daily mood ratings and emotional check-ins; physical symptoms such as cramps, fatigue, bloating, and headaches; personal journal entries and notes; relationship feelings, notes, and emotional patterns; food craving preferences and the recommendations generated for you; and your exercise, dietary, and sleep preferences as provided during onboarding.

1.3 Usage Information

We collect basic information about how you interact with the App, such as how frequently you open the App and which features you use. This information helps us improve the App experience. We do not track your geographic location.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes: to calculate your current cycle phase and provide personalized guidance related to nutrition, movement, and rest; to generate insights about your mood, symptom, and relationship patterns across different phases of your cycle; to power our AI-based food recommendation feature, which uses your craving preferences and current cycle phase to suggest suitable options; to deliver daily wellness reminders through push notifications tailored to your cycle phase; to enable you to optionally share selected information with a partner through the App's sharing feature; and to maintain, improve, and develop new features for the App.

3. How We Store and Protect Your Data

Your data is stored on secure, encrypted cloud servers. All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted in transit using industry-standard TLS encryption. Data stored on our servers is encrypted at rest.

Certain information, such as your daily log entries and notification preferences, is also cached locally on your device to enable offline functionality and faster loading times.

We implement commercially reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect your personal information. However, no method of electronic transmission or digital storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. AI-Powered Features

Petal's Craving Decoder feature uses artificial intelligence to generate food recommendations. When you use this feature, your craving preferences (such as texture, flavor, and temperature selections) and your current cycle phase are sent to a secure AI processing service. This data is used solely to generate your recommendation and is not retained by the AI service for model training or any other purpose.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, license, or trade your personal information to any third party. We do not display advertisements within Petal, and no advertiser has access to your data.

Your data may be disclosed only in the following limited circumstances: when you explicitly choose to share information with a partner using the App's sharing feature, in which case you maintain full control over which categories of data are included through granular sharing settings; to trusted service providers who assist us in operating the App, solely for the purpose of providing the App's functionality and subject to strict confidentiality obligations; or when required to comply with applicable law, regulation, legal process, or enforceable governmental request.

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Access and Deletion

You may access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. You can delete your entire account and all associated data directly from the Settings screen within the App. When you delete your account, we permanently remove all of your data from our servers, including your profile information, daily logs, journal entries, cycle data, craving history, and sharing preferences.

6.2 Notification Preferences

You may enable or disable push notifications at any time through your device's system settings.

6.3 Rights for EU/EEA Residents

If you reside in the European Union or European Economic Area, you have additional rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). These include the right to access your data, the right to rectification, the right to erasure, the right to restrict processing, the right to data portability, and the right to object to processing. Our legal basis for processing your personal data is your explicit consent, which you provide when you create your account and agree to this Privacy Policy. You may withdraw your consent at any time by deleting your account. To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

6.4 Rights for California Residents

If you are a California resident, you have rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know what personal information we collect and how it is used, the right to request deletion of your personal information, and the right to not be discriminated against for exercising your privacy rights. We do not sell personal information as defined under the CCPA.

7. Children's Privacy

Petal is not intended for individuals under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under the age of 16. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected data from a user under 16, we will take prompt steps to delete that information. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information to us, please contact us immediately.

8. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account remains active or as needed to provide you with the App's services. If you delete your account, all associated data is permanently removed from our servers within thirty (30) days. Locally cached data on your device is cleared immediately upon account deletion.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may revise this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will provide notice within the App prior to the changes taking effect. Your continued use of the App following the posting of a revised Privacy Policy constitutes your acceptance of such changes. If you do not agree to the revised policy, you should discontinue use of the App and delete your account.
